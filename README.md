# Mix Listener

A small website for listening to audio files from the `songs` folder.

## Upload To Webspace

Upload these items to your webspace:

- `index.php`
- `styles.css`
- `songs/`

Then put your audio files into `songs/` and open the site in your browser.

Your webspace needs PHP enabled because `index.php` scans the `songs/` folder when the page loads.

Supported audio extensions: `.aac`, `.aiff`, `.flac`, `.m4a`, `.mp3`, `.ogg`, `.opus`, `.wav`, `.webm`.
